Manicure and Pedicure

Manicure and Pedicure are a mostly cosmetic beauty treatment for the fingernails, hands and feets. A manicure usually consists of filing and shaping the free edge of nails, pushing and clipping any nonliving tissue, treatments with various liquids, massage of the hand, and the application of fingernail polish.

SPA can guide you through your future manicure and pedicure experiences.
